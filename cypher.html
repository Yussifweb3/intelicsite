Welcome to Intelic Institute of Technology

We are thrilled to announce a new chapter in our journey. YAJ NETWORK has transitioned to Intelic Institute of Technology, a premier online educational platform designed for learners from preschool through university. Our platform, developed by Intelsofft (Intelligent Solutions for Future Transformation), marks a significant advancement in educational innovation and accessibility.

Our mission is to offer high-quality education to learners around the globe with a variety of curricula tailored to meet diverse needs. Free courses will start later this month, and our comprehensive programs will launch next year. The Intelic Institute is committed to providing top-notch education through a state-of-the-art online platform and app.

In partnership with leading organizations such as Loop and iStream, we are set to deliver exceptional educational experiences. Our website and app are designed with cutting-edge technology, offering an intuitive interface, interactive features, and a seamless learning journey.

We encourage you to explore our platform, experience our offerings, and provide feedback to help us continually improve. Your insights are crucial as we aim to deliver the best possible educational experiences. Join us in this exciting new venture and be part of the future of education!
