Language Arts

Enhance your reading, writing, and comprehension skills in English and other national languages.

Mathematics

Explore mathematical concepts and problem-solving techniques.

Science

Understand the principles of biology, chemistry, and physics through interactive experiments.

History

Study past events and understand the historical contexts that shape our present and future.

Geography

Learn about the Earth's landscapes, environments, and the human impact on our planet.

ICT and Computing

Develop skills in information and communication technology and understand the basics of computing.

Art

Express yourself through various forms of art including drawing, painting, and sculpture.

Music

Learn to appreciate and create music through various instruments and vocal techniques.

Physical Education

Engage in physical activities to promote fitness, health, and teamwork.

Society

Study the structure and function of societies, including government, economics, and culture.

International

Gain a global perspective by understanding international issues and diverse cultures.
