Admission Process

Welcome to Intelic Basic Online School, where quality education is accessible from the comfort of your home. Our admission process is designed to be simple and straightforward, ensuring that every student can start their learning journey with ease.

We accept students for the following programs:

  • Primary School (Grades 1-6)
  • Junior High School (Grades 7-9)

How to Apply

  1. Step 1: Complete the Online Application Form
    Fill out our online application form with accurate information about your child and family.
  2. Step 2: Submit Required Documents
    Upload the necessary documents, including birth certificate, previous school records, and any additional documents requested.
  3. Step 3: Schedule an Interview
    Once your application is reviewed, our admissions team will contact you to schedule an online interview with your child.
  4. Step 4: Receive Admission Decision
    After the interview, you will receive an email with the admission decision within 5-7 business days.
  5. Step 5: Pay Tuition Fees
    If admitted, you will receive instructions on how to pay the tuition fees. Once payment is confirmed, your child will be officially enrolled.
  6. Step 6: Start Learning
    Your child will receive access to their online classes and resources. We look forward to supporting your child's educational journey!
