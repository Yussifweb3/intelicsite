Religious Activities at Early Learning School

Our Religious Activities

We believe in nurturing the spiritual growth of our students. Our school offers various religious activities to help students develop their faith and values. Here are some of our key religious activities:

Morning Prayer

Start your day with a peaceful and reflective morning prayer session, helping students to center themselves and set a positive tone for the day.

Bible Study

Our Bible study sessions provide an opportunity for students to explore and understand the teachings of the Bible in a supportive group setting.

Interfaith Dialogues

Encouraging understanding and respect for diverse faiths, our interfaith dialogues create a platform for open and meaningful conversations about different religious beliefs.

Religious Festivals

We celebrate various religious festivals throughout the year, providing students with an opportunity to learn about and participate in different cultural and religious traditions.
