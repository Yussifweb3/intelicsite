High School Courses

Mathematics

Algebra

Explore the fundamentals of algebra, including equations, inequalities, and graphing. Develop problem-solving skills through practical applications and interactive exercises.

Geometry

Study the properties and applications of geometric shapes and theorems. Learn about angles, circles, triangles, and proofs through engaging activities and visual aids.

Calculus

Delve into the world of calculus, covering limits, derivatives, and integrals. Apply calculus concepts to real-world problems and develop a strong mathematical foundation.

Science

Biology

Discover the complexities of living organisms, from cell biology to ecosystems. Engage in hands-on experiments and explore the principles of genetics, evolution, and ecology.

Chemistry

Learn about chemical reactions, the periodic table, and molecular structures. Participate in lab work to understand chemical properties and processes in a practical setting.

Physics

Explore the principles of physics, including mechanics, electromagnetism, and thermodynamics. Use experiments and simulations to understand physical phenomena and their applications.

Humanities

History

Examine historical events, cultures, and civilizations. Analyze primary sources and develop an understanding of how past events shape the present and future.

Literature

Read and analyze literary works from various genres and periods. Discuss themes, characters, and writing styles to enhance your critical thinking and interpretive skills.

Philosophy

Explore fundamental questions about existence, knowledge, and ethics. Engage with classical and contemporary philosophical texts to develop your reasoning and analytical abilities.

Languages

English

Improve your proficiency in English through reading, writing, and speaking exercises. Study grammar, vocabulary, and composition to enhance your communication skills.

Spanish

Learn the basics of Spanish, including grammar, vocabulary, and pronunciation. Practice speaking and listening through interactive activities and cultural immersion.

French

Gain a foundation in French language skills, including speaking, reading, and writing. Explore French culture and literature to deepen your understanding of the language.
